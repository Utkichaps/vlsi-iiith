Procedure

Components which will be used for this:PMOS, NMOS, Input, Output, Vdd, GND, Capacitor, Joint, OJoint.


Steps (Can also refer to the diagram below):
  1. Select XOR Gate from the option on the top of the page, under the heading.
  2. Connect two pairs of PMOS (4 in total) in parallel and connect that in series between Vdd and 3 input OR. To do that:
    • Place two PMOS side by side
    • Connect drain of first PMOS to output of a Joint and connect that to the drain of the second PMOS.
    • Connect Vdd to the input to the input of the above mentioned Joint.
    • Connect source of first PMOS to an OJoint and connect that to source of second PMOS.

    • Place two more PMOS side by side below the above connections.
    • Connect drain of first PMOS to output of a Joint and connect that to the drain of the second PMOS.
    • Connect the input of the above mentioned Joint to the OJoint of the first parallel connection of PMOS.
    • Connect source of first PMOS to an OJoint and connect that to source of second PMOS.
    • Connect the above mentioned OJoint to the first input of the 3 input OR gate.
  3. Connect two pairs of NMOS (4 in total) in series and connect that in parallel between 3 input OR and GND. To do that:
    • Place four NMOS in the same way the PMOS has been placed.
    • Connect the second input of the 3 input OR gate to an OJoint.
    • Connect the above OJoint to the Drain of the top two NMOS.
    • Connect the source of one of the NMOS to an OJoint and connect that to the Drain of the NMOS below it.
    • Do the above step for the two NMOS on the other side.
    • Connect the source of the bottom two NMOS to the Output of a Joint.
    • Connect the input of the above mentioned Joint to GND.
  4. Connect the GND to the Input of a capacitor.
  5. Connect the output of the capacitor to the third input of the 3 input OR.
  6. Connect the output of the 3 input OR to an Out component.
  7. For the inputs, put 4 In components on the workspace.
  8. Each In component should be connected to one PMOS and one NMOS. For this, follow the diagram given above.
