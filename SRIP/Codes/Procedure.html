Procedure

Components which will be used for this:PMOS, NMOS, Clock, Delay, Input, Output.


Steps:
  1. Select positive level pass transistor from the list on the top of the page.
  2. Connect source of PMOS with drain of NMOS and connect input pin serially to it.
  3. Connect drain of PMOS with source of NMOS and connect output pin serially to it.
  4. Connect clock to the delay and connect that to gate of PMOS (clock bar).
  5. Connect clock to gate of NMOS (clock).
  6. Press the simulate button and observe the graph.

Components which will be used for this:Pass transistor, Clock, Delay, Input, Output, OR gate.


Steps:
  1. Select multiplexer from the list on the top of the page.
  2. Place two pass transistors on the workspace.
  3. Connect clock to delay (clock bar) and connect it to gate of NMOS (NGate) of 1st pass transistor and gate of PMOS (PGate) of 2nd pass transistor.
  4. Connect an Input pin to each transistor's input.
  5. Connect clock to gate of PMOS of first transistor and gate of NMOS of 2nd transistor.
  6. Connect the outputs of the two transistors to an OR gate.
  7. Connect the output of the OR gate to an output pin.
