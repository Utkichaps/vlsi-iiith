Simulator layout

Firstly you have to select which simulation you want to run by choosing one of the options which is in the list area under the main heading of the page.
The simulator consists of a menu on the left. You can load the pass transistor circuits by clicking on one of the circuits given there or you could make it yourself.
To know how to build the circuits on your own, click on procedure, a new window will appear on the right side of the workspace where you can refer to the instructions.
Once you have completed the circuit, either by choosing the See circuit options or building your own, click on the simulate button to simulate the waveform, which can be seen right below the workspace of the circuit simulator.
Clear workspace can be clicked to remove the simulation as well as the circuit.

How to use the Simulator:

  • Choose a component from the toolbox and move it to the right.
  • Connections can be made clicking the input node and dragging it to an output node(or vice versa).
  • To disconnect two components click on the input node.
  • To remove a component drag it back to the toolbox.
  • To edit the device name double click the label.
  • To see the inner workings of a device, double click it.
  • Once you are done with the circuit, click on simulate to simulate the waveform.
