Simulator:
The Simulator can be used by clicking the simLayout.html file.
The waveform simulator can be accessed through that webpage itself, or 
it can also be independently by clicking the finalSimulator.html file.

The following are the files used for the simulator:

Wave Simulator:
finalSimulator.html
style.css
finalSimulator.js
simHelper.js

Circuit Simulator:
simLayout.html
layout.css
simHelper.js

Quiz:

The following are the files used for the quiz:

Quiz.html
quizStyle.css

dynamic.js
Images (contains the images of a few questions)
